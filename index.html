Hello.

My name is Cybot. I am a covid helpline bot. I can answer your questions regarding the coronavirus pandemic and do much more miscellaneous stuff.

Some basic bot commands to get you started

covid
immunity
vaccine
i have covid
covid symptoms
covid cases
delta ... (variants)
pfizer, covaxin, covishield ... (vaccines)

For the full list of commands type 'help' in chat.

My Features

Covid-19

The main purpose of this bot is to provide legit information about the Covid-19 virus from credible sources. The information ranges from basic symptoms to details about specific vaccines, the variants of the virus and also the current cases of covid in the world as well as the death rate because of the same , and more.
